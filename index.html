Technology to make a difference!

THE VISION OF MASH PROJECT IS TO BUILD A COMMUNITY OF TECHNOLOGICALLY ENABLED SOCIAL CHANGE MAKERS.

What we do?

We aim to engage, equip and empower people to create a sustainable habitat through power of technology.

What is MASH Project?

OUR MISSION
We are an organization committed to building a global society on the premise of sustainability, a society where sustainable living is not limited to protecting our natural environment but, also includes overall human development and the social well being of a person.
We plan to achieve this by harnessing the power of 21st century technological breakthroughs such as Mobile Applications, information dissemination through Social Media, interactive community-building events like Hackathons and an ever increasing range of other possibilities that technology has to offer.
